SYSTEM COMPONENTS

1] NodeMCU ESP8266: The NodeMCU ESP8266 serves as the central hub of our Smart Irrigation System. This powerful microcontroller, equipped with Wi-Fi capabilities, allows for seamless communication and data exchange between the various components of the system.
2] Soil Moisture Sensor Module: The soil moisture sensor module plays a crucial role in measuring the moisture levels in the soil. By accurately detecting soil moisture levels, this sensor provides real-time data to the system, enabling precise irrigation control.
3] Water Pump Module: The water pump module is responsible for delivering water to the crops based on the moisture levels detected by the soil moisture sensor. Controlled by the system, the water pump ensures that crops receive the optimal amount of water for healthy growth.
4] Relay Module: The relay module acts as a switch, controlling the operation of the water pump. By interfacing with the NodeMCU ESP8266, the relay module enables the system to activate or deactivate the water pump as needed, ensuring efficient water distribution.
5] DHT11 Sensor: The DHT11 sensor measures temperature and humidity levels in the environment. This data is valuable for monitoring environmental conditions and optimizing irrigation schedules to suit the specific needs of different crops.
6] Connecting Wires: Connecting wires are used to establish electrical connections between the various components of the system. These wires ensure reliable communication and power distribution within the Smart Irrigation System
